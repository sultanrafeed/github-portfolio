01 — About

A builder with a researcher's instincts.

I'm a Software Engineer at SELISE Digital Platforms, where I lead development of OpenClaw and expand MCP server tooling — driving architecture decisions across agentic AI and enterprise automation workstreams.

On the research side, I work on advancing NLP — particularly trustworthy and responsible AI, LLM alignment and evaluation, prompt optimization for robust instruction-following, and retrieval-augmented generation in multilingual and low-resource settings. I'm also drawn to medical AI applications of NLP — clinical text and decision support — with the broader goal of building transparent, fair language technologies.

I hold a BSc in Computer Science Engineering from North South University and have published at ICPR 2024, IEEE Big Data 2024, IJCNN 2025, and the Q1 journal Intelligence-Based Medicine.
